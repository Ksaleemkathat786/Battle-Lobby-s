Refer and Earn

Home / Refer and Earn

Battle Lobby offers you a great opportunity to earn money by referring your friends. The more people you refer, the more rewards you earn!

Every time your referred friend joins the app and plays a match, you earn a reward instantly. This is a perfect way to earn while enjoying your favorite games.

How Refer Works?

  • Go to your profile and copy your referral code.
  • Ask your friends to download the Grind Battle app.
  • Once they enter your referral code and play their first match, you receive a bonus!

How Rewards Work?

  • You can earn between ₹10 to ₹50 for every successful referral.
  • The more your referred user plays, the more rewards you receive.
  • Use your earned bonus to join matches or purchase in-game items.

So what are you waiting for? Start referring and start earning today!
